MineCraft Sword

​For this piece I built a giant sword that belongs to a man name Trunks from the anime series Dragon Ball Z. The cultural significance in this piece is that it ties to the Japanese anime culture in which I love and enjoy. This sword has a lot of meaning because this sword originally belonged to someone else name Tapion who was the original owner. He later passed down his sword to a younger version of trunks as a token of their friendship. From that point on, after Tapion disappeared, Trunks kept the sword with him for the rest of his life in remembrance of his friend.

Close Project

​After building the sword in minecraft, I used the software mineways to extract the object from the game to later put it in file to 3-D print. The left sword was the first one I printed. I little bit detailed but scruffy in some areas. The right one was the final one I printed which came out much more cleaner after adjusting the settings on the 3-D printer program.
